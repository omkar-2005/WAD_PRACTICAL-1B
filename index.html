Features Of Coffee

"Junk coffee" isn't a formal term but generally describes coffee that's low-quality, artificial, overly sweetened, or made with unhealthy additives, contrasting with natural, high-quality brews; think heavily processed instant mixes or sugary concoctions loaded with syrups and artificial flavors, serving as a cheap or quick fix rather than a genuinely enjoyable coffee experience. It often lacks the balanced aroma, flavor, and mouthfeel of good coffee, focusing instead on intense sweetness or caffeine delivery
